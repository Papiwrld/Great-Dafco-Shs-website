About Us

We are a Private Senior High School operating under the Free SHS policy, fully registered with the Registrar General's Department and accredited by all relevant educational bodies including the Ghana Education Service (GES), NaSIA, WAEC, CHOPSS, and others.

Our commitment to academic excellence has reflected in our remarkable record, achieving over 95% pass rate in the WASSCE examinations for the past five (5) years. This success is driven by our team of well-trained and seasoned tutors, who are dedicated to nurturing students both academically and morally.

We provide a holistic learning environment that blends discipline, quality teaching, and modern learning resources, ensuring our students are well-prepared for higher education and future opportunities.

In addition to our mainstream SHS programs, we also run two series of remedial classes for WAEC (Nov/Dec) examinations every year, giving both past and present students the opportunity to improve their grades and achieve their academic goals.

We are located at Donyina in the Ejisu Municipal, providing a serene learning environment for academic excellence.
